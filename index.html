Crystal Collector Game!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. This is your target number.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on one of these four crystals,
you will add a certain amount of points to your total score.

You can only win the game by matching your total score to a random number.
You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal becomes known once you click on it.
Each time the game begins, the values of each crystal change.

Good luck!
